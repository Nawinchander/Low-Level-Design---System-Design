Interview Answer Framework for LLD Questions

Whenever asked to design a system:

Step 1: Clarify requirements

Example:

scale?
concurrency?
extensibility?
Step 2: Identify entities

Example for parking lot:

Vehicle
Spot
Ticket

Step 3: Define responsibilities

Use single responsibility

Step 4: Identify relationships
inheritance?
composition?
Step 5: Apply patterns
Strategy
Factory
Observer
Step 6: Discuss tradeoffs

Example:

“In-memory is fast but not distributed”

8. Most Important Interview Tips
Tip 1:

Always talk before coding
Interviewers evaluate thinking.

Say:

“First I’ll identify entities and relationships.”

Tip 2:

Use interfaces/abstraction

Instead of:

class PaymentService {}

Use:

class PaymentStrategy {}

Shows extensibility.

Tip 3:

Mention SOLID explicitly

Say:

“I’m using strategy pattern to satisfy OCP.”

This sounds senior-level.



Tip 4:

Discuss scaling
Even in LLD ask:

DB?
caching?
distributed state?
